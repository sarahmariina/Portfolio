Tangoterapi

- Webdesign, UI and usersegment

In this project our assignment was to develop digital products for a specific usersegment. Our primary focus was design process. We were given the task to construct a design challenge; “How do we get more people to join tango?” We started our research phase, with deskresearch/observations. Our idea for a solution was to use Tango as a couples therapy trial, where couples would be able to work on their relationsship through dance lessons. My primary role in this project was to collect data for target group analyzing.
