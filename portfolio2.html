TeamZumba

Usersegment, optimizing and Iterations

In this project we were given the project of another group. Our task was to optimize and further develop their project. As we saw limitations to their initial concept, we decided to completely redesign the webside and concept. We decided to offer Zumba as teambuilding event for companies who wished to bring the coworkers together. We began a new researchphase, with deskresearch/observations/intervies.

My role in this project was to administer the design process, and illustrate our iterations in the process.
